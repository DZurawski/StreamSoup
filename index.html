What should I watch tonight? Is that on Netflix? Who's in it? Is it any good? Have my friends seen it? Did they like it?
Watching TV isn't supposed to be this hard. Leave the answers to those questions to us, all you have to do is enjoy. StreamSoup - all your television needs in one place.

Stream Soup is an application that aims to help users by recommending TV shows based on the streaming services they use, their previously watched shows and their general entertainment preferences. The problem Stream Soup is attempting to solve is the disconnect between different streaming services and the content that they provide. A user should be able to simply check Stream Soup to find new/existing TV shows as opposed to logging in to several streaming websites such as Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime Video, Crunchyroll, HBO etc.
